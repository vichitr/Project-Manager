Welcome to Student Teacher Project Manager!

If you already have an account, go ahead and log in. If you are new to Student Teacher Project Manager, get started by creating a student account or a teacher account or a teacher assistant account.

What's this about?

Student Teacher Project Manager solves the problem of managing course mini projects and even more. Teachers just need to update the information on the web and students need to do everything online. In this application, users can sign up as a student or a teacher or a reviewer. Teachers can add courses and students can submit projects based on their interests. Reviewer account is for teacher assistants who review the projects submitted by students.
